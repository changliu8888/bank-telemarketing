Summary of findings
•	The rate of “yes” response is highest when the bond rate is low, and when total employment is low.  
•	The conversion rate is higher when consumer price index is low and when consumer confidence index is low.  
•	Anyone who has had earlier successful conversion will have a much higher chance of saying yes.  
As for the fitting strategies, it is found that the decision tree classifier is the most accurate and economical.
